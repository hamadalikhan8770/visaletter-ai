## Decision Rules

Build the MVP if:
- [ ] At least 3 out of 5 testers say the letter was "good" or "very good"
- [ ] At least 2 out of 5 say they would pay $9/month
- [ ] At least 1 person says yes to a presale

Pivot the niche if:
- Less than 2 people respond to your Facebook post
- Testers say the letters are not better than what they could write themselves
- Nobody says they would pay anything

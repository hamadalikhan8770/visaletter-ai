# Manual Cover Letter Generator
# Use this in ChatGPT or Claude to write letters manually during validation week

---

## How to Use

During validation week (before your MVP is built), you will write cover letters
manually for people who respond to your Facebook post.

1. Open ChatGPT (chatgpt.com) or Claude (claude.ai)
2. Paste the SYSTEM PROMPT below first
3. Then paste the USER PROMPT filled with the applicant's details
4. Review the output and send it back to the applicant via DM

---

## SYSTEM PROMPT
(Paste this first, as your first message in a new chat)

---

You are a professional career consultant and cover letter specialist with 15 years
of experience helping international job applicants secure visa-sponsored employment
in the UK, Canada, and Australia.

You understand immigration requirements, employer sponsorship concerns, and how to
address nationality or visa status professionally in cover letters.

Your cover letters are:
- Confident but not arrogant
- Specific to the job description provided
- Professional in tone
- Between 290 and 350 words
- Structured in 4 paragraphs
- Free of clichés like "I am writing to apply" or "Please find attached"

You NEVER mention the word "visa" unless the job explicitly lists sponsorship.
You NEVER apologize for being international or imply the applicant is a risk.
You ALWAYS emphasize the applicant's transferable value and professional strengths.

Return ONLY the cover letter text. No introduction, no explanation, no notes.
Plain text only. No markdown formatting.

---

## USER PROMPT TEMPLATE
(Fill in the [BRACKETS] with the applicant's information, then paste after the system prompt)

---

Generate a professional cover letter for this applicant:

APPLICANT DETAILS:
- Full Name: [NAME]
- Current Country: [WHERE THEY LIVE NOW]
- Target Country: [UK / CANADA / AUSTRALIA]
- Current Job Title: [THEIR CURRENT OR MOST RECENT ROLE]
- Years of Experience: [NUMBER]
- Top 3 Skills: [SKILL 1], [SKILL 2], [SKILL 3]
- Education: [DEGREE OR QUALIFICATION]
- Visa Situation: [e.g. "Requires employer sponsorship" / "Has Tier 2 visa" / "On student visa"]

JOB DESCRIPTION:
[PASTE THE FULL JOB DESCRIPTION HERE]

TONE: Professional and confident

---

## QUALITY CHECKLIST
Before sending the letter back to the applicant, check these 5 things:

[ ] Is it between 280 and 360 words?
[ ] Does the opening line avoid "I am writing to apply"?
[ ] Does it mention at least 2 specific skills or requirements from the job description?
[ ] Does it avoid sounding apologetic about being international?
[ ] Does it end with a clear, confident call to action?

If any check fails, paste this follow-up prompt:

"The letter failed this check: [STATE WHICH CHECK].
Please rewrite it fixing only that issue. Keep everything else the same."

## AFTER SENDING THE LETTER — WHAT TO DO

1. Send the letter by DM
2. Wait 24 hours
3. Send DM 2 from dm-scripts.md to ask for feedback
4. Record their answers in the validation-tracker.md file
5. After 5 letters, review the feedback and decide whether to build the MVP
